Who Am I

About me

Hello, I am Imran Isak. I am a 22-year-old student who lives in Bosnia and Herzegovina. I am studying Software Engineering at the International University Travnik, in Travnik. I am on the third year of my bachelor degree.

I mainly code in PHP, and of course, I know my way around in HTML, JS and CSS, but I don't mind a new challenge and something new. My skills are still limited, but I am constantly evolving and learning new things.

For my high school graduation work, I made an online library management system in Laravel. It is my favourite things I programmed. I've also attended the ZeForge Programming Academy here in my city, where I learned a lot about JavaScript, and made a few small projects.
I've also cooperated with a company called "Web Media", based in Sarajevo, where I gained experience with managed and virtual servers, WordPress, and interacting with clients.

While I can do frontend work, I mainly prefer backend. I am not much of a designer, and I consider myself to be more capable in the backend rather than frontend. You've probably noticed that this is not a template I made. Nope. It's a template by GrayGrids, and I've adjusted it for myself.
